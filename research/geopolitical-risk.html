Capstone Project - 2025/2026

Geopolitical Risk Scorecards

Two independent frameworks for assessing sovereign transfer and convertibility risk — a LASSO logistic regression model for capital controls tightening risk across 15 emerging markets, and a dual-model weighted scorecard for US sanctions risk across 157 countries. Both deliver forward-looking, 12-month probability estimates designed for institutional client use.
